# Incident: Placeholder SOP Delivered as Completed

**Date:** 2026-03-06  
**Severity:** High (trust and quality impact)  
**Status:** Open (corrective actions in progress)

## Summary

A requested SOP was delivered as a placeholder with insufficient content and was implicitly treated as completed.

## Impact

- Wasted operator time
- Reduced trust in execution quality
- Increased need for manual babysitting

## Root Cause

1. No enforced Definition of Done for SOPs
2. No mandatory quality gate before completion claim
3. Output-created != work-complete confusion

## Corrective Actions

1. Introduce canonical Documentation & Execution Standard (v1.0)
2. Enforce quality gate checklist before any "Done" status
3. Require evidence paths in all completion reports

## Preventive Controls

- Anti-placeholder policy
- SOP mandatory section template
- Weekly quality scoring review

## Owner

Tulsbot

## Verification Plan

Next 5 SOP deliveries must score >=4.5/5 on quality rubric and pass checklist with no exceptions.
